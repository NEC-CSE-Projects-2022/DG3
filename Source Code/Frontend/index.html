Welcome to Train Delay Prediction System

The project “Leveraging Operational and Environmental Data for Train Delay Prediction via Deep Learning Models” aims to develop a reliable and user-friendly system that accurately predicts train delays. By analyzing various factors such as past delay records, weather conditions, and route information, the system provides valuable insights that help passengers and railway authorities plan better.This model improves accuracy compared to traditional methods by using advanced deep learning techniques that can recognize hidden patterns in data. The results show that the proposed system can significantly reduce uncertainty in train schedules and enhance the overall efficiency of railway operations.
